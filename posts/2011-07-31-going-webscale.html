I recently decided to clean up my personal online act in a more modern and mobile friendly manner. I started with this newer, shorter, domain which is pretty nice after 10 years of typing westcoastlogic.com. Buidling a new personal site gave me the excuse I needed to author another nodejs based blog library too. This time I opted for JavaScript's sexy little sister CoffeeScript.

Check out this snippet of router logic:

app = module.exports = require './config'

app.get '/', (req, res) -> 
    res.render 'index'

app.get '/about', (req, res) -> 
    res.render 'about'

app.get '/contact', (req, res) -> 
    res.render 'contact'

app.listen process.env.PORT || 3000
